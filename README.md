<p id="description">Idea is a powerful and intuitive notes app developed using Flutter that aims to enhance your note-taking experience. Whether you're jotting down quick thoughts making to-do lists or organizing your ideas Idea provides a user-friendly interface and a wide range of features to streamline your note management.</p>

  
  
<h2>üßê Features</h2>

Here're some of the project's best features:

*   Dark Mode & Night Mode: Idea understands your preference for different viewing modes. It offers both dark mode and night mode options allowing you to seamlessly switch between interfaces that are easy on your eyes in various lighting conditions.
*   CRUD Operations for Notes: With Idea managing your notes has never been easier. The app allows you to perform Create Read Update and Delete (CRUD) operations on your notes. Jot down new ideas edit existing notes mark tasks as complete or delete notes you no longer need - all with a few simple taps.
*   CRUD Operations for Note Folders: Idea goes beyond just notes. It empowers you to organize your notes efficiently by offering CRUD operations for note folders. You can create manage and delete folders providing a structured way to categorize your notes based on different topics or projects.
*   Nested Folders: To take organization a step further Idea enables you to create folders within folders. This hierarchical structure allows you to create a truly personalized organization system making it easy to access related notes whenever you need them.
*   Search Functionality: Finding specific notes within your extensive collection is a breeze with Idea's robust search functionality. Quickly locate notes by entering keywords or phrases ensuring that no important information gets lost in the sea of notes.
*   Hive Local Database: Idea leverages the power of Hive a lightweight and efficient local database for Flutter applications. This ensures that your notes and folders are stored securely on your device while maintaining smooth performance even with large amounts of data.
